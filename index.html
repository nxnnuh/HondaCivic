Eighth Gen Honda Civic Upgrades

This page is to show possible upgrades for a Honda Civic eighth generation.


What is an eighth gen?

An eighth generation Honda Civic is a vehicle produced from 2005 to 2012. The civic came in various body styles such as sedan, coupe and hatchback. Its design was meant to be "futuristic" with a digital speedometer and two-tier dashboard.

Whats good about an eighth gen?

The eighth generation Honda Civic is praised for its modern design, exceptional fuel efficiency and strong reliability. The car is compact and ideal for manueuvering while still offering a spacious cabin.

Reasons why you should love this car:

  • Fuel Efficiency: 26-36 miles per gallon, $45 - $55 to fill up gas tank
  • Low maintenance: oil changes, brakes and tires are affordable and easy to service
  • Crash test: cabin held up well in crash tests and earned top marks in frontal and side crashes
  • Trunk Space: smaller cars typically have trunk issues, not the civic
