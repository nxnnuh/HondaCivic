Eighth Gen Honda Civic Car Modifications

This page is to show possible Body Shop Modifications for a Honda Civic eighth generation.



Quality of Life Upgrades

  • Touchscreen Head Unit: Many manufacturers create aftermarket head units to install into the 8th generation civic. This means you can have carplay and bluetooth now built into the car.
  • Backup Camera: Along with the new head unit you can add a fully functioning backup camera.
  • LED headlights: This allows you to keep up with newer trends of bright lighting and due to the Honda Civic build you can easily replace these yourself.

Outer Car Modifications

  • Aftermarket Wheels: Instantly boosts look of car and adds to the sleek design. Also improves performance.
  • Aftermarket Chassis Parts: This includes a front lip and skirt, a rear spoiler and side and rear rain covers. Makes your car look insanely different.
  • Vinyl: Almost everything in this car can be wrapped in vinyl, including the outside. This makes customization easy.
